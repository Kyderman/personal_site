My Role

At Betting In The Know, I am the sole developer and have been since starting on the application in around November 2014. Since then, the site has grown from a simple horse advice sharing and betting calculation tool, in to a fully fledged subscription site with over 100 paid members, generating around 15,000 page views per week. Not only do I develop, but I also manage customer service queries as well as webmaster tools, managing the application via Heroku and the myriad of plugins it provides.
